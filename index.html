Indus Public School

Indus Public School is spread over a vast lush green campus on National Highway-10. 30 minutes drive from Delhi Border. The School is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi up to XII level providing education in Medical, Non-Medical, Commerce Streams. Every initiative, effort at the upcoming institute is single-mindedly focused on these three basic values. So while the young minds go through the process of learning, it will automatically, continually and effortlessly imbibe them, to give the individual an dynamic personality.Our vision has led us to adopt a persuasive and learner-centric teaching methodology that focuses on an Explore-Experience-Implement model of learning that is practiced at the level of organization. Read more...

Education

Indus Group of Institutions has grown in to a symbol of comprehensive education. It is a ‘Bodhi tree’- a source of knowledge and education. The group is pioneer of quality education, providing education from Nursery to XII based on the time tested and result oriented CBSE syllabus. The group institutions offer under graduate and post graduate courses in the field of Engineering & Technology, Management, Arts, Commerce, Science, Nursing and Teacher Education etc. Our group strives to provide at par Global education systems in the rural and urban areas of India. The group endeavors to emerge as a role model in the field of quality education. The future plans include providing quality education avenues by establishing institutions of international repute.
